Cave Night

Stay a night at a cave specially designed and research for a perfect night in the outdoors. If you want to experience something new, this is the trip you want!

Canyon Adventure

Our classic option. Spend a day rafting in the rapids and then have a picnic while enjoying the view of the canyon! This can be a one or two night experience. Send us a message for more information.

For those who only want to relax and rest, we have a special offer for you: our all-included cabins. You can take a couple of days rest and enjoy our natural spa included in this package.
